# Contractor Ecosystem Plan

## Six-Month Bench Goal

Build a managed contractor network with redundancy by trade:

- 2 roofers
- 2 concrete contractors
- 2 tile installers
- 2 carpenters
- 2 painters
- 2 plumbers
- 2 electricians
- 2 cleaning/turnover crews
- 2 landscaping/exterior crews

## Contractor Profile Fields

Each contractor should have:

- business name
- public slug
- trades
- service areas
- license number
- insurance status
- portfolio photos
- preferred job types
- availability status
- QR/intake URL
- response speed
- communication notes
- pricing tendencies
- trust tier
- internal vetting notes

## Network Process

1. Add contractor profile.
2. Verify license and insurance status.
3. Add trades and service areas.
4. Upload 3-10 past project photos.
5. Generate public profile and QR intake link.
6. Route 1-3 low-risk quote opportunities.
7. Track response speed, clarity, pricing, reliability, and client experience.
8. Promote, pause, or demote based on real behavior.

## Estimate Intelligence Relationship

Shelter Prep should not estimate only around one owner/operator workflow.

The app estimates with:

- general market labor rate
- urgency labor multiplier
- contractor-specific rate when available
- owner-operator/self-perform fallback
- overhead
- coordination margin
- risk buffer

That keeps the company useful while the contractor network grows.
